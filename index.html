Nick Leigh

Portrait & Automotive Photographer

Hey, I'm Nick! I'm a photographer and student from Orange County CA. I'm also a fan of cars, tech, food, and having a good time! I've been a photographer for about 4 years and invested in my first camera in 2017. It wasn't long until i was drawn to portrait photography (the photographing of people). Shoot after shoot, I discovered the endless possibilities to creating digital art from the emotions and lifestyles of my friends through my eyes. Now I do this for a number of others as well! Being a college student who is majoring in business, I am looking forward to taking my skills as a content creator to new heights and further growing my brand!

Currently, I am a second-year student at UCR. I am studying business as I hope to create and grow my own photography business in the future. By majoring in business, I will be able to better understand the means necessary to manage my own business. I will learn to better account for the money I earn and how to invest into gear, a studio, and even into properties or stocks to further increase my income!

A goal I have with my passion photography is to become a well-known photographer and artist and be able to photograph for various fashion and automotive magazines. I want to be the one people call when they want a simple Instagram photo, but I also want to be the one people call when they want wedding photos to keep forever.
